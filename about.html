Our Mission

Founded to expand access to diplomacy and global politics, the Sacramento Valley Model United Nations organization serves as a local way for students in the region to engage in cost-effective, competitive dialogue. Entirely student-led and supported by the volunteers of college/university Model UN club members, we provide opportunities for youth to engage in global affairs, strengthen their skills in public speaking, and grow into thoughtful, informed leaders.
